Ranveer Brar

Cheff

Ranveer Brar, a culinary expert, is the youngest among India's top 5 chefs. At the age of 25, he was the youngest official culinary specialist to have ever worked with a five-star hotel in India. Judge on Masterchef India, author, restaurateur, maker of culinary films, and benefactor. He has opened restaurants in India and overseas, as well as curating the menus of a number of others.

Vikas Khanna

Cheff

Vikas Khanna, a 45-year-old Michelin-starred Indian chef, restaurateur, and cookbook author, is one of India's most well-known figures, as well as a global celebrity. His youthful passion for cooking led him to work with the Taj, Oberoi, Welcomgroup, and Leela Group of Hotels, as well as with some of the world's most famous chefs, including Gordon Ramsay, Bobby Flay, and Jean-Georges Vongerichten.

Sanjeev Kapoor

Chef

Sanjeev Kapoor is India's most famous chef. He is a chef extraordinaire, the creator of the successful TV channel FoodFood, the host of the Khana Khazana cuisine programme on television for over 17 years, the author of over 150 best-selling publications, a restaurant, and a multiple culinary award winner. He is achieving his goal of making Indian food the best in the world and encouraging women to become self-sufficient through the power of cooking.
